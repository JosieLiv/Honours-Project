Insecure Design

Insecure Design stems from failure to consider all possible threats and abuse cases
resulting in an underestimation of the level of security needed for an application.
As attack vectors are often overlooked, certain authorisation mechanisms and security
principles can be absent from the final design.

In this lab, you will have access to the web application for Book Nook, where users can browse, buy
and wishlist books. However, the site has been deployed too early and the developers were very sloppy.
In this login page, the error messages are too detailed which a user could exploit.
